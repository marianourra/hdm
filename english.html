About Hijos del Mar

In the vast, infinite embrace of the ocean, we find our home and our identity.

We are "Hijos del mar", a group of friends woven by the waves, the wind and the salt. We are those who feel the call of the sea in every whisper of the waves, in every reflection of the sun on the water.

Being a "Hijo del mar" is not a title reserved only for those who have founded this journey, but for everyone. who love the sea with an unwavering passion. It is for those who find peace in its immensity, adventure in its mysteries, and a deep connection in its eternal dance.

Who have started this path are simply the guides on a journey that is much bigger than ourselves and we invite you to share it with us. Are you ready? Let's go!

The longest wave in the world

Where are we going?

Puerto Chicama, located on the northern coast of Peru, is a place that resonates in the hearts of surfers around the world. This small coastal town is famous for having the longest wave in the world, a natural wonder that can offer courses of more than two kilometers. With a rich history linked to the sea, Chicama is a destination that not only offers adrenaline, but also a deep connection with nature and local culture. This is the place where surfing dreams come true, in an environment of incomparable beauty and serenity.
